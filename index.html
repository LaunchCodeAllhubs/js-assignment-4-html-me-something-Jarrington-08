The 80's: Super Mario Bros. 3

Super Mario Bros. 3 came out in 1988, quickly becoming one of Nintendo's most popular titles. I mean who doesn't like flying around as Racoon Mario!?
(Trivia: The original name for this is Tanooki Mario in Japan based on the Japanese racoon-dog.) Not only did the game feature tons of variety in worlds
and enemies, you could always warp your way past difficult parts using those magical warp whistles!
